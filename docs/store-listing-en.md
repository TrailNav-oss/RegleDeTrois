## Full description
Proportio is your everyday calculation tool: proportions, percentages, conversions and recipe scaling.

★ RULE OF THREE
Fill in 3 values, the 4th is calculated automatically. Perfect for proportions, dosages and everyday math. Searchable history included.

★ RECIPES
Create recipes with ingredients. Change the number of portions and all quantities recalculate. Quick multiply buttons (×0.5, ×2, ×3). Adjusted mode: change one ingredient and the others adapt proportionally. Share your recipes with one tap.

★ PERCENTAGES
5 calculation modes:
• % of a number (what is 15% of 200?)
• Variation (from 80 to 100, what %?)
• Increase (200 + 20% = ?)
• Decrease (200 - 20% = ?)
• Find the % (30 is what % of 200?)
Presets for common percentages (5%, 10%, 15%, 20%, 25%, 50%).

★ CONVERSIONS
Weights (g, kg, oz, lb), volumes (ml, cl, L, cup, fl oz, tbsp, tsp) and temperatures (°C / °F). Real-time conversion. Swap units with one tap.

★ FEATURES
• Clean, intuitive interface
• Dark mode
• French / English
• Metric and imperial units
• 100% offline — your data stays on your device
• Free with ads, or Premium for ad-free and unlimited recipes
